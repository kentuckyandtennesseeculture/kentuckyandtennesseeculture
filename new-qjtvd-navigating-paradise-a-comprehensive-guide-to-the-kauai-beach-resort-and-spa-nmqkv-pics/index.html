FAQs: Unraveling the Resort’s Mysteries

Q: Are there specific amenities for families with children?

A: Yes, the resort caters to families with a dedicated children’s pool, a playground, and supervised activities for various age groups.

A: The resort’s restaurants are equipped to cater to various dietary needs, including gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan options. It is recommended to inform the restaurant staff of any dietary restrictions upon arrival.

A: The resort offers shuttle services to nearby attractions, and car rentals are available for those who prefer independent exploration.
