Exploring the Surroundings: Proximity to Attractions

The Marriott Kauai Beach Club Resort map also highlights the resort’s proximity to local attractions, making it easy for guests to plan day trips and explore the island’s natural wonders.

  • Poipu Beach: Renowned for its calm waters and golden sands, Poipu Beach is a short walk from the resort, offering excellent swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing opportunities.
  • Spouting Horn: This natural blowhole, formed by volcanic activity, is a short drive from the resort and provides a captivating display of water erupting from the rocks.
  • Waimea Canyon: Known as the "Grand Canyon of the Pacific," Waimea Canyon offers breathtaking views and hiking trails, a short drive from the resort.
