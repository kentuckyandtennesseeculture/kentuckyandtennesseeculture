The digital landscape is increasingly filled with tools designed to help us understand the world around us. Unveiling the Past: Exploring the Features of Ad Astra 2

Ad Astra 2, a product of the Kansas Historical Society, is a digital map that allows users to navigate through the state’s rich history, focusing on the period between 1854 and 1910. This period, marked by significant westward expansion and the establishment of Kansas as a state, holds immense historical value. The map, meticulously crafted using historical data and resources, provides a visually compelling and interactive platform for exploring the following:

  • County Boundaries and Political Divisions: Ad Astra 2 displays the evolving county boundaries throughout the chosen timeframe. This feature allows users to trace the changes in political divisions and observe how the state’s administrative structure developed.
  • Railroads and Transportation: The map showcases the vital role of railroads in shaping the state’s growth. Users can visualize the expanding network of railway lines, understanding how they connected communities, facilitated trade, and contributed to the state’s economic development.
  • Townships and Cities: The map highlights the establishment and growth of various townships and cities across Kansas. Users can observe the emergence of urban centers, the development of infrastructure, and the evolving landscape of the state’s population distribution.
  • Historical Events and Figures: Ad Astra 2 incorporates information about significant historical events that occurred in Kansas, such as the Civil War, the Dust Bowl, and the development of the cattle industry. Users can access details about these events and explore their impact on the state’s social and economic fabric.
  • Demographic Data: The map provides insights into the changing demographics of Kansas during the chosen period. This includes data on population growth, ethnic composition, and migration patterns, offering a detailed picture of the state’s evolving population landscape.
